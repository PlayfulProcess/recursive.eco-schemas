THE MAD TEA PARTY

BY LEWIS CARROLL
A 5-MINUTE STORY

ALICE FOUND A TEA PARTY.
THE MAD HATTER, THE MARCH HARE,
AND THE DORMOUSE SAT AT A BIG TABLE.
"NO ROOM! NO ROOM!" THEY SAID.

1

"WHY IS A RAVEN LIKE A WRITING DESK?"
ASKED THE HATTER.
"I DON'T KNOW," SAID ALICE.
"I DON'T KNOW EITHER!" SAID THE HATTER.

2

THE HATTER HAD A BROKEN WATCH.
IT TOLD THE DAY BUT NOT THE TIME.
"IT IS ALWAYS SIX O'CLOCK," HE SAID.
"IT IS ALWAYS TEA-TIME."

3

"TELL US A STORY!" THEY SAID TO THE DORMOUSE.
"ONCE THERE WERE THREE SISTERS...
THEY LIVED IN A WELL..."
THE DORMOUSE FELL ASLEEP.

4

"THIS IS THE SILLIEST TEA PARTY EVER!"
SAID ALICE.
SHE GOT UP AND WALKED AWAY.
"I AM NEVER COMING BACK!"

5
THE END
