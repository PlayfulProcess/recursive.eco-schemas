THE LOBSTER DANCE

BY LEWIS CARROLL
A 5-MINUTE STORY

"LET ME SHOW YOU THE LOBSTER DANCE!"
SAID THE MOCK TURTLE.
THEY ALL DANCED ON THE BEACH.
"WILL YOU WALK A LITTLE FASTER?"
SANG THE MOCK TURTLE.

1

THE MOCK TURTLE SANG A SONG:
"BEAUTIFUL SOUP, SO RICH AND GREEN,
WAITING IN A HOT TUREEN!"
HE LOVED SOUP VERY MUCH.

2

THEN SOMEONE SHOUTED:
"THE TRIAL IS STARTING!"
THE GRYPHON GRABBED ALICE'S HAND.
"COME ON!" HE SAID. "LET US GO!"

3
THE END
