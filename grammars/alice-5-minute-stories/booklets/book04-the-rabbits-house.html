ALICE IN WONDERLAND
BOOK 4

THE RABBIT'S HOUSE

BY LEWIS CARROLL
A 5-MINUTE STORY

THE WHITE RABBIT SAW ALICE.
"MARY ANN! GO GET MY GLOVES!"
ALICE WAS NOT MARY ANN.
BUT SHE WENT TO HIS HOUSE.

1

IN THE HOUSE, SHE DRANK A BOTTLE.
SHE GREW SO BIG SHE GOT STUCK!
HER ARM WENT OUT THE WINDOW.

2

"BILL! GO DOWN THE CHIMNEY!"
A LITTLE LIZARD CAME DOWN.
ALICE KICKED HIM RIGHT BACK UP!

3

AT LAST ALICE ATE SOME PEBBLES.
THEY WERE REALLY LITTLE CAKES!
SHE GOT SMALL AND RAN AWAY.
SHE MET A VERY BIG PUPPY.

4
THE END
