ALICE IN WONDERLAND
BOOK 1

DOWN THE RABBIT HOLE

BY LEWIS CARROLL
A 5-MINUTE STORY

ALICE WAS SITTING BY THE RIVER.
SHE WAS VERY, VERY BORED.

1

THEN A WHITE RABBIT RAN BY.
"OH DEAR! I SHALL BE LATE!"
SAID THE RABBIT.

2

ALICE JUMPED UP AND RAN AFTER HIM.
THE RABBIT JUMPED INTO A BIG HOLE.

3

ALICE JUMPED IN TOO.
SHE FELL DOWN, DOWN, DOWN.
PAST SHELVES AND MAPS AND JARS.

4

SHE LANDED IN A HALL WITH MANY DOORS.
ON A TABLE THERE WAS A TINY BOTTLE.
IT SAID: "DRINK ME."

5

ALICE DRANK IT AND GOT VERY SMALL!
THEN SHE ATE A CAKE THAT SAID "EAT ME."
AND SHE GREW VERY, VERY BIG!

6
THE END
