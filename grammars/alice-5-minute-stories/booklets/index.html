HOW TO PRINT

  1. Click on a book above to open it
  2. Press Ctrl+P (or Cmd+P on Mac) to print
  3. Set orientation to Landscape
  4. Set margins to None
  5. Turn on Background graphics
  6. Print on both sides (flip on short edge) if your printer supports it
  7. Fold each sheet in half — you have a booklet!
