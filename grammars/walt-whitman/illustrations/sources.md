*All images public domain. Downloaded 2026-04-21.*

This folder contains every illustration currently referenced by `grammar.json`, plus a few extras. Each file below lists its origin, creator, year, rights basis, and alternate sources.

All files are safe to mirror into Cloudflare R2 for the recursive.eco platform. When mirroring, keep the filenames or rename consistently and update `grammar.json` `image_url` fields.

## Known gaps

- **Harleigh Cemetery tomb** — no stable PD institutional photo. Using Blake "Death's Door" as symbolic substitute (the engraving inspired Whitman's tomb design).
- **Brooklyn ferries, 1850s** — no direct LoC image for that specific decade. Phase II emergence currently uses Brady portrait; alternate: Currier & Ives New York harbor lithograph or Bachmann Brooklyn panorama 1846.
- **Brooklyn printer, 1830s** — no Whitman-specific printshop. Consider generic period composing-room engraving from LoC P&P.
- **"Song of Myself" manuscript** — the complete 1855 manuscript is lost. Substitutes: WWA 1855 title-page image or surviving notebook fragments (LoC Harned Collection).
