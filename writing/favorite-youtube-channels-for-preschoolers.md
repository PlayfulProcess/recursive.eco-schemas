A place for me to share others content that work for us as a family and to share what I have been producing myself. 

First, I want to introduce you to the video that set the guidelines of screen time for my kid. The basic principles are 1) scenes last longer than 3 seconds; 2) they look as close as possible to the real world; 3) they look as close as possible to Mr Rogers' Neighborhood (even though my kid is not very interested in that).
