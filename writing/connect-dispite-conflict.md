I find the following tool especially useful when there is conflict. When we connect deeply with our feelings and needs, I find that a curiosity is born about what the other people we might be in conflict are feeling and needing. In NVC we say that needs are never in conflict, only strategies. By connecting with this realm of feelings and needs, we can then think about better strategies that can release the conflict.
