## Learnings

1. **Ragnarök is unique**: The ONLY mythology where the gods definitively LOSE. This makes the Norse cycle deeply honest — endings are real, AND new beginnings follow. Both truths held simultaneously.

2. **Loki is the most complex trickster**: Neither good nor evil — he gifts the gods with treasures AND causes Baldr's death. Modern readings (especially post-Marvel) tend to oversimplify him.

3. **Odin as shaman**: His hanging on Yggdrasil (self-sacrifice to himself), eye-sacrifice, and rune-gaining parallel shamanic initiation rites globally. This connects directly to the Tibetan bardo teachings.

4. **The Green arc is essential**: Most retellings end at Ragnarök. But Völuspá continues — the world rises GREEN from the sea. This is the hope the Norse tradition offers, and it's the emotional payoff.
