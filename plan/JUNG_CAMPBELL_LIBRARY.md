## THE VISION

Imagine the recursive.eco library with these additions. A seeker could:

1. **Follow the descent**: Egyptian Book of the Dead > Eleusinian Mysteries > Tibetan Bardo > Pistis Sophia > Swedenborg's Heaven and Hell -- five cultures' maps of what happens when the ego dies.

2. **Follow the feminine**: Golden Ass (Isis) > Homeric Hymns (Demeter) > Songs of Kabir (the beloved) > Song of Songs > Revelations of Divine Love -- the goddess thread through all traditions.

3. **Follow the opus**: Mirror of Alchimy > Hidden Symbolism > Kybalion > Jungian Archetypes -- alchemy as the map of becoming whole.

4. **Follow the hero**: Myth of the Birth of the Hero (Rank) > Mabinogion > Arabian Nights > Popol Vuh > Kalevala -- the thousand faces across cultures.

5. **Follow the crack**: Chuang Tzu > Meister Eckhart > Plotinus > Zarathustra > Leaves of Grass -- the mystics who saw through the veil.

This is the library where Jung and Campbell would meet, argue, and agree: that the psyche speaks in images, that myth is the dream of the species, and that every story ever told is, finally, about waking up.
