# The Seed Library: 100 Books as a Tree of Knowledge

**Date:** 2026-03-04
**Purpose:** First 100 public domain texts for recursive.eco-library, curated as intellectual roots that converge into the thinkers who matter to us: Bayo Akomolafe, Josh Shrei, Marsha Linehan, John Gottman, Sue Johnson (EFT), Vanessa Andreotti, Christopher Kelty.

Each book is classified by **shelf** (adaptive theme), **lineage** (which modern thinker it feeds), and **Gutenberg ID** where available.

---

## The Tree of Emergence

```
                        ┌─── Bayo Akomolafe (Yoruba + Process + Decolonial)
                        │
Eastern Wisdom ─────────┼─── Marsha Linehan (Zen + Dialectics + Radical Acceptance)
                        │
African Cosmology ──────┼─── Vanessa Andreotti (Decolonial + Indigenous + Both-And)
                        │
Mythopoetic/Enchantment┼─── Josh Shrei / Emerald (Tantra + Myth + Animism)
                        │
Love & Emotion ─────────┼─── Gottman + Sue Johnson (Attachment + Emotion Science)
                        │
Commons & Cooperation ──┼─── Christopher Kelty (Open Source + Recursive Publics)
                        │
Wonder & Imagination ───┴─── The Kids (Alice + Fairy Tales + Stories that heal)
```
